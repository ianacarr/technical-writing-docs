User Guide

A guide on how to unbox, start, and use your laptop. This guide should give you a place to start and build off of and provide a comprehensive introduction into working with your laptop.

Troubleshooting

The Troubleshooting section helps you fix common laptop problems like power issues, screen flickering, sound failures, and WiFi connectivity. Each topic includes a visual reference and a list of practical solutions you can try on your own. Whether your laptop won’t turn on or your cursor keeps jumping, this guide is designed to walk you through quick fixes and restore functionality without needing professional help.

FAQs

The FAQs section answers common questions about laptop setup, features, and everyday use. It’s ideal for new users who want to understand how to connect to WiFi, personalize settings, manage battery life, or explore built-in apps. If you're unsure about technical terms like SSD or GPU, or need help creating a Microsoft account, this section provides clear explanations to help you feel confident using your device.
